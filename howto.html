The ultimate shoulder workout

  1. Head to your local gym.
  2. Head to the free weights.
  3. Grab weights that match your strength
    • Choose a weight you can control
  4. Sit on a bench.
  5. Lift weights up to shoulder height 12 times
    • Keep arms straight without bending elbows
    • Keep your back straight
  6. Rack the weights.

Why it is important to workout shoulders.

Working out your shoulders strengthens the muscles involved in almost all upper-body movements, improving stability and reducing injury risk.
Strong shoulders support good posture and prevent rounded shoulders. They enhance performance in sports and daily activities while also boosting other lifts like bench presses and pull-ups.
Additionally, well-developed shoulders improve your overall physique and balance the upper body.

Benefits of Lateral Raises

  • Strengthens shoulder muscles (deltoids)
  • Improves posture
  • Increases upper-body stability
  • Enhances performance in other lifts and sports
